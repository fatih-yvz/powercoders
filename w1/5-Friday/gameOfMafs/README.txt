Welcome in Game Of Mafs. It stands for Game of Mustafa-Ari-Fatih-Saadettin

You imagine , you are new visitor in Switzerland and you want to go special place in Switzerland. 
This is our final destination for you :
- Zermatt
Your starting point is :
- Zurich
Use command "cd" to move within places and "cat" to read the information about those places. To reach the destination you can check the hint and choose the right directory. You win the game if you can reach the destination if you can use "cd" only 3 times. We can't wait to see you in Zermatt!
Tips : 
use ls to explore to surrounding, cd to move to another directories, and cat to read hints.
